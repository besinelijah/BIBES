About Us


iBookMoYan! is a hotel booking website
dedicated to offering hotel services to
people searching for comfort and
luxury. We are here to accommodate to
everyone interested in our services, and
we promise to fully cater to your needs.

The 4-storied hotel is a beautiful
combination of traditional grandeur
and modern facilities. Exclusive guest
rooms are furnished with a range of
modern amenities such as television
and internet access. Direct-dial phones,
safe, and wake-up call facilities are also
available in the rooms.
